"You don't!" Fern stepped backwards, closer to the edge. "Nobody does-and that's normal! Nobody can understand each others pain, which is why we keep hurting each other! We keep hurting each other, to see if anyone, anyone can understand."


Fern stopped, the shaking in their voice vanishing into the darkening sky. "I didn't hurt anyone. That was my mistake. Because then nobody understood me. Nobody understood how much I missed them. Missed my old life. How much I wanted to be loved by them."


The wind was howling, as Fern's plaid jacket swayed in the wind. The light from the street below shone on their back, as they turned back to face the street below.


You had to think quick. Was there anything, anything you could use or tell them to get them to get away from that ledge?


You didn't know.
